About Me

Where Tech Meets Art

Two decades of software development taught me precision. A lifetime of painting taught me vision. Together, they create something unique.

I spent 20 years building software solutions before transitioning to visual content creation. That technical foundation gives me a unique advantage: I understand both the creative and technical sides of digital content.

Based in Melbourne, I work with startups, healthcare organizations, and small businesses to create content that performs. Whether it's a social media campaign, video production, or brand visuals, I approach every project with the same attention to detail I learned in software development.

My art practice keeps me creatively sharp. With over 200 original paintings created, I bring that same artistic sensibility to commercial work, ensuring every piece is not just functional, but beautiful.

Visual Content Social media graphics, video editing, motion graphics, brand design
Technical Skills HTML/CSS/JS, WordPress, Adobe Creative Suite, Final Cut Pro
